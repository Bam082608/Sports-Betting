## Betting Protocol for Tier 2

### When to Bet:
- ✅ Favorable matchup (weak defensive team)
- ✅ Build combo tickets with Tier 1 stars
- ✅ Use for role player combos (Ticket 3)

### Stake Sizing:
- **Tier B ($1.25):** Strong matchup, verified patterns
- **Tier C ($0.75):** Medium confidence, testing

### When to Skip:
- ❌ Playing vs elite defensive teams (CAR, BOS, NJ, VGK)
- ❌ Back-to-back situations
- ❌ Unclear line combinations

## Promotion Criteria (Tier 2 → Tier 1)

A Tier 2 player can be promoted to Tier 1 when:
1. SOG average reaches 5+
2. Concentration reaches 25%+
3. Verified over 10+ game sample
4. Claude confirms promotion

---

## Demotion Criteria (Tier 2 → Skip)

A Tier 2 player should be removed when:
1. SOG average drops below 3
2. Concentration drops below 15%
3. Injury or role change

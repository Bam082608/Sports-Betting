## Criteria for Tier 1

| Metric | Requirement |
|--------|-------------|
| **SOG Average** | 5+ shots per game |
| **Shot Concentration** | 25%+ of team shots |
| **Consistency** | 8+ of 10 games hit 4+ SOG |

## Betting Protocol for Tier 1

### Always:
- ✅ Bet **2P+3P splits** (not full game)
- ✅ Use **Tier A stakes ($2.50)**
- ✅ Bet every game regardless of matchup
- ✅ Include in Ticket 1 and Ticket 2

### Never:
- ❌ Skip a game because of "bad matchup"
- ❌ Reduce stake size
- ❌ Bet full game instead of period splits

---

## Verification Process

To move a player to Tier 1:
1. Gemini pulls last 10 game stats
2. Calculate SOG average (must be 5+)
3. Calculate concentration (must be 25%+)
4. Verify consistency (8 of 10 games 4+ SOG)
5. Claude reviews and confirms

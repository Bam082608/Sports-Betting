## 1. THE CORE PRINCIPLE
**"Match structure to narrative. Don't bet against yourself."**

**The Error:** Betting an OVER in your Anchor while betting an UNDER as insurance/hedge.
*   *Result:* You hedge yourself out of profit. If the game goes Under, your insurance wins but your Anchor dies. If Over, Anchor wins but insurance dies.

*   You are betting **Under 3.5/4.5 Insurance** separately.

**Structure:**
*   Team Spread (+1.5/+2.5)
*   Star Player 2+ SOG
*   **UNDER 6.5 or UNDER 7.5** (Absolute Ceiling)
*   *PROHIBITED:* Any Over leg.

### VARIANT B: OFFENSIVE ANCHOR üî•
**Use When:**
*   Narrative is offensive (Backup goalies, weak D, high total).
*   You are **NOT** betting Under insurance (or are confident enough to fade it).

**Structure:**
*   Team Spread (+1.5/+2.5)
*   Star Player 2+ SOG
*   **OVER 5.5 or OVER 6.5** (Absolute Floor)
*   *PROHIBITED:* Any Under leg.

### VARIANT C: MIDDLE ANCHOR (The "Window") ü™ü

*   **RARE USE ONLY (<20% of games).**

**Structure:**
*   Team Spread (+1.5/+2.5)
*   Star Player 2+ SOG
*   **OVER 4.5** (Floor)
*   **UNDER 7.5** (Ceiling)

## 3. DECISION CHECKLIST
Before placing an Anchor, check your Insurance bets for that game:

1.  **Am I betting Under Insurance?**
    *   YES -> Must use **VARIANT A (Defensive)**.
2.  **Am I betting Over Insurance?**
    *   YES -> Must use **VARIANT B (Offensive)**.
3.  **Am I betting NO Insurance?**
    *   Match Anchor to your narrative (Offensive or Defensive).

> **Rule:** Never have an Anchor leg that directly contradicts your Insurance bet.

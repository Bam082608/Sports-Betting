## 2.  SYSTEM PHILOSOPHY

### 2.1 Narrative Coherence
Every bet placed must answer the question: **"What story am I betting on?"**

**PROHIBITED:**
- Betting Over AND Under in same game
- Contradictory spread positions (+1.5 AND +2.5 stacking)
- Random leg accumulation without thesis

**REQUIRED:**
- Clear game narrative identification
- All legs supporting unified thesis
- Mathematical non-contradiction

### 2.2 Tiered Risk Allocation
Capital protection through structured allocation:

```
TIER 1 (Core): 50-60% of bankroll allocation
TIER 2 (Extended): 25-30% of bankroll allocation
TIER 3 (God Tickets): 5-10% of bankroll allocation
TIER 4 (Hedges): 10-15% of bankroll allocation
```

### 2.3 Narrative Accuracy Over Outcome
Success is measured by **narrative accuracy**, not individual bet outcomes.

A night where:
- Narrative was CORRECT but props missed = **Learning opportunity**
- Narrative was WRONG but bets hit = **Lucky, not skillful**

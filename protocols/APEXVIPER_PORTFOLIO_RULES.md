## 2. EXPOSURE LIMITS (TITANIC RULE ENFORCEMENT)

### The Titanic Rule
**"Never put so many eggs in one basket that a single sinking destroys everything."**

### Team Exposure Limits

| Metric | Max Limit | Reason |
|--------|-----------|--------|
| **Tickets featuring same team** | 2 max | Diversification across slate |
| **Same team in parlays** | 50% of parlay legs max | Avoid correlated collapse |
| **Single game exposure** | 25% of nightly stake | No single-game dependency |
| **Same outcome dependency** | Never | (e.g., Team ML + Team spread) |

### Player Exposure Limits

| Metric | Max Limit | Reason |
|--------|-----------|--------|
| **Same player across all tickets** | 2 appearances max | Avoid single-player dependency |
| **Players from same team in parlay** | 2 max per parlay | Reduce correlation risk |
| **Same prop type (e.g., SOG)** | 70% of total props max | Mix prop types |

### Titanic Rule Compliance Checklist

Before placing bets, verify:
- [ ] No team appears in more than 2 tickets
- [ ] No player appears in more than 2 tickets
- [ ] No single game represents >25% of nightly stake
- [ ] No parlay has >50% legs from same team
- [ ] Exposure spread across 3+ games minimum

## 3. DIVERSIFICATION MANDATES

### Minimum Requirements

| Requirement | Rule | Enforcement |
|-------------|------|-------------|
| **Team Representation** | Min 3 different teams per night | Block bets if not met |
| **Game Spread** | Bets across min 2 games | Block single-game nights |
| **Prop Type Mixing** | At least 2 different prop types | SOG + Points, or SOG + Assists, etc. |

### Recommended Prop Type Mix

| Prop Type | Recommended % | Notes |
|-----------|---------------|-------|
| **Shots on Goal (SOG)** | 40-60% | Core system focus |
| **Points** | 20-30% | Tier 1 stars only |
| **Goals** | 10-20% | Selective, high-odds |
| **Assists** | 10-20% | Correlation plays |
| **Saves** | 5-10% | Goalie-specific |

## 6. SLATE SIZE ADJUSTMENTS

### Small Slate (2-4 games)

| Rule | Adjustment |
|------|------------|
| Max teams per ticket | 2 (down from 3) |
| Total exposure | Reduce to 2% max |
| Lottery tickets | Skip (limited options) |
| Diversification | Accept 2 teams minimum |

### Medium Slate (5-8 games)

| Rule | Adjustment |
|------|------------|
| Standard rules apply | No changes |
| Target | 3-4 different teams |
| Full portfolio | Foundation + Layers + Lottery |

### Large Slate (9+ games)

| Rule | Adjustment |
|------|------------|
| Max games to analyze | 5 (avoid paralysis) |
| Diversification | Target 4+ teams |
| Lottery opportunities | Can increase slightly |
| Hedge opportunities | More options available |

## 7. CORRELATION AWARENESS

### High Correlation (AVOID combining)

| Combination | Risk |
|-------------|------|
| Team ML + Team spread | Same outcome dependency |
| Team total over + Player points | Correlated success |
| 3+ players same team in parlay | Single-game collapse risk |
| Game over + Both team totals over | Redundant exposure |

### Low Correlation (SAFE to combine)

| Combination | Benefit |
|-------------|---------|
| Player SOG from different teams | Independent outcomes |
| Player props across different games | Diversified risk |
| Team ML + Different team props | Uncorrelated success |

### Negative Correlation (HEDGE value)

| Combination | Use Case |
|-------------|----------|
| Player SOG over + Game under | Hedges scoring expectation |
| Team ML + Opponent props | Partial hedge if team loses |

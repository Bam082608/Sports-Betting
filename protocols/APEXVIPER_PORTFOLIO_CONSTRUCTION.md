## 1. THE TITANIC RULE
**The Law:** Never load too many eggs in one basket.

- No diversification across slate

## 3. THE PYRAMID LOGIC

**Standard Ticket Types:**  
See [TICKET-ARSENAL-v2.md](/playbook/TICKET-ARSENAL-v2.md) for complete library of tested betting structures including:
- The Anchor (foundation)
- Volume Stack (offensive exploitation)
- Mirror (hedge strategy)
- Black Swan (insurance)
- God Mode (lottery)
- Superstar Ladder (elite targeting)
- Specialist (depth scoring)
- Time-Hedged Anchor (experimental)

### Foundation (60-70% of exposure)
**Purpose:** Stable, high-probability bets.
- Single-game props on verified stars
- Low correlation between bets
- Higher confidence, lower variance

### Layers (20-30% of exposure)
**Purpose:** Moderate risk/reward.
- 2-3 leg parlays across games
- Cross-game combinations
- Medium correlation plays

### Apex (5-10% of exposure)
**Purpose:** High reward, acceptable loss.
- Large parlays (4+ legs)
- "God tickets"
- Full acceptance that these will lose often

## 5. THE CORRELATION MATRIX

### High Correlation (AVOID stacking)
- Same team, same game, same outcome dependency
- Example: Team ML + Team spread + Team total over

### Low Correlation (SAFE to combine)
- Different teams, different games
- Example: Player A props (Game 1) + Player B props (Game 2)

### Negative Correlation (HEDGE opportunities)
- Opposite outcomes that offset
- Example: Props over + Game under

## OVERVIEW

Goalie confirmation is CRITICAL for NHL betting. A last-minute goalie change can invalidate entire betting strategies. This protocol establishes a three-stage verification process to ensure you never bet on outdated goalie information.

---

## 1. THREE-STAGE VERIFICATION PROCESS

### Stage 1: Initial Check (Morning - 10:00 AM ET)
**Purpose:** Establish baseline goalie expectations

### Stage 2: Morning Skate Confirmation (Midday - 12:00 PM - 1:00 PM ET)
**Purpose:** Verify goalies based on morning skate reports

### Stage 3: Final Confirmation (Pre-Game - 2-3 Hours Before Puck Drop)
**Purpose:** Lock in confirmed starters before bet placement

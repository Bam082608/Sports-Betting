## Critical Rules

### For Stars (Tier 1)
- Always bet **2P+3P splits** (not full game)
- Use **Tier A stakes ($2.50)**
- Bet every game regardless of matchup

### For Role Players
- Require **2+ SOG** minimum
- Build combo tickets with stars
- Use **Tier B-C stakes**

### Hedge Protocol
- When betting opponent D-men props, hedge with **Under total shots**
- Protects against low-event games

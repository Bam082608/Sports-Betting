## 1. THE HOME UNDERDOG LAW
**The Law:** NHL home underdogs cover the puck line at a significantly profitable rate.

**The Stat:** 63.9% cover rate on Home +1.5 puck line

**Why It Works:**
- Market overvalues road favorites
- Home ice advantage underpriced
- Last change (line matching) creates matchup control
- Crowd energy provides measurable boost
- Parity in NHL makes 2-goal margins rare

---

## 2. THE CORE STRATEGY

### Primary Play: Home +1.5 Puck Line
| Element | Specification |
|---------|---------------|
| **Bet Type** | Puck Line (spread) |
| **Selection** | Home team +1.5 goals |
| **Market** | Regulation (no OT) |
| **Edge** | 63.9% historical cover rate |

### Why +1.5 Over Moneyline
- Moneyline requires win
- +1.5 covers with win OR 1-goal loss
- Higher hit rate = more consistent returns
- Lower variance than ML underdogs

## 5. SITUATIONS TO AVOID

### When Home Underdog Edge Weakens
| Situation | Why | Action |
|-----------|-----|--------|
| **Home team on B2B** | Negates rest advantage | AVOID or downgrade |
| **Home team missing starter** | Goalie edge lost | Check backup quality |
| **Road team on extended rest** | Fresh legs equalizer | Standard analysis only |
| **Heavy public on home dog** | Value already priced in | Check line movement |

### Red Flags
- Home underdog line moving AGAINST them
- Sharp money reported on road favorite
- Goalie mismatch (backup vs elite starter)
- Injured star player for home team

## ğŸ”§ PLATFORM-SPECIFIC WARNINGS

### FanDuel
- **Issue:** Decimal point is small and light gray
- **Solution:** Zoom in browser to 125% before bet entry
- **Trick:** Look at the cents, not the dollars ($X.95 â†’ focus on "95")

### DraftKings
- **Issue:** Amount field auto-fills from last bet
- **Solution:** ALWAYS clear field and re-type from scratch
- **Warning:** Don't use "Bet Again" feature for different amounts

### BetMGM
- **Issue:** Comma separators can be confused with decimals
- **Solution:** Read amount backwards: 95, decimal, 0, dollar sign
- **Example:** "$1,095" could be misread as "$1.095"

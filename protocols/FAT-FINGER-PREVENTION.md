## üéØ HIGH-RISK SITUATIONS

### When to Use ENHANCED PROTOCOL (Triple-Check)

**Trigger Conditions:**
1. **Bet amount >5% of bankroll**
   - Standard: $0.50-$3.00 on $569 bankroll
   - High-risk: >$28.45 (requires enhanced protocol)

2. **After 3+ consecutive bets**
   - Muscle memory can cause autopilot errors
   - Take 30-second break between every 3 bets

3. **Late night betting (after 10 PM)**
   - Fatigue increases error rate
   - Double the pause time to 4 seconds

4. **Emotional states**
   - After a big win (overconfidence)
   - After a loss (tilt, rushing)
   - During game action (distraction)

5. **Decimal-heavy amounts**
   - $0.95, $1.25, $2.50 etc.
   - Easy to miss decimal or add extra zero

## üîß PLATFORM-SPECIFIC WARNINGS

### FanDuel
- **Issue:** Decimal point is small and light gray
- **Solution:** Zoom in browser to 125% before bet entry
- **Trick:** Look at the cents, not the dollars ($X.95 ‚Üí focus on "95")

### DraftKings
- **Issue:** Amount field auto-fills from last bet
- **Solution:** ALWAYS clear field and re-type from scratch
- **Warning:** Don't use "Bet Again" feature for different amounts

### BetMGM
- **Issue:** Comma separators can be confused with decimals
- **Solution:** Read amount backwards: 95, decimal, 0, dollar sign
- **Example:** "$1,095" could be misread as "$1.095"
